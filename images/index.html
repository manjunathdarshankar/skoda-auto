Skoda Rapid

Skoda Rapid is a 5 seater Sedan with the last recorded price of Rs. 6.99 - 14.25 Lakh. It is available in 27 variants, 1498 to 1598 cc engine options and 2 transmission options : Manual and Automatic.

Skoda Mounte Carlo

The Mounte Carlo is Skoda India's entry-level crossover model that is known for its refinement, build quality, and performance. It gets two petrol engine options and three gearbox options. The 1.0-litre TSI engine produces 109bhp and 175Nm of torque, while the 1.5-litre TSI motor produces 148bhp and 250Nm of torque.

Skoda Kodiaq

It is available in 3 variants, with an engine of 1984 cc and a choice of 1 transmission: Automatic. Kodiaq has an NCAP rating of 5 stars and comes with 9 airbags. Skoda Kodiaq has a ground clearance of 140 mm and is available in 6 colours. Users have reported a mileage of 13.32 kmpl for Kodiaq.
